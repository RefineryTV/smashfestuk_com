We’re on the way to the Awards Ceremony!

Published by Lindsay on November 28, 2016

 

We’re on our way to Bristol, feeling very excited! SMASHfestUK has been shortlisted for a national university public engagement award, and we’re off to the NCCPE Engage Conference where there’ll be an exhibition of all the finalists, and interviews to decide the ultimate winner. We’re honoured to be there, in the company of such wonderful other projects, and honoured that the work of all the SMASHfestUK team, the Lewisham community, Middlesex University and the University of Greenwich and the support from all our other partners and sponsors has been recognised. Thank you all!
