SPACE PLAGUE VR Adventure

A virulent disease has arrived on Earth via multiple meteorite strikes. It causes extreme, ‘zombie-like’ symptoms in everyone infected.

Scientists and Engineers are rushing to find a cure and create a vaccine, but they need everyone’s help…

Join the ‘Emergency Response Team’ and help SAVE THE WORLD!

SIGN UP IN THE BOX TO THE RIGHT, to download the VR game FREE.

This is SMASHfestUK’s first VR game, based on our live immersive show – ‘Space Plague’ https://flic.kr/s/aHsmLBCu6H

We’re co-designing, as always, so the game is being launched as a free, fully playable ‘beta’ prototype, with you as co-designers. We’d love you to play and let us know what you think, and any ideas and suggestions for the next version, on the form accompanying the download.

You’ll need access to an Oculus Rift (or Quest 2 to cable in to PC) to join us as part of our ‘Emergency Response Team’ VR Co-Design Community. Please follow the Oculus guidelines for use.
